Prime - Product Information Management

×
  • Prime manages all Product Information: Structured Data, Unstructured Data (Romantic Copy, Images and Documents), and Retails.
  • Division → Department → Class → Subclass (Actually, nine hierarchy levels are allowed, only two levels are required)
  • Collection → Item → SKU (Collection is optional)
  • Inventory Sets and Selling Sets with set pricing and child sku price proration.
  • Not Stocked and Not Sellable SKUs to support fees, fixtures, supplies...
  • Lean fixed Structured Data. Unlimited User Defined Data (UDD) configured at any level in the product hierarchy. Fifteen UDD data types.
  • Data Cloning throughout the Prime.
  • Rules based distribution of Product Information to legacy and third-party systems. All Retail Calculus applications access Product Information in real-time using secure APIs.

Product Explorer

×

In-Store Web Site - Has every capability that best-in-class eCommerce sites have but delivers all the SKUs and Product Information that you want Sales Associates to see including detailed and drillable product and purchase order availability. Detailed product status.

  • Uses familiar eCommerce site conventions. Easy to learn and understand.
  • Fine tuned for Sales Associates to allow them to focus on the customer and selling.
  • Rich information which can include videos helps to make the Sales Associate "The Product Expert."

Made to Order - Guided customer special order creation from Stock Plus to full Custom. Generates purchase order ready SKUs. Configured in Prime and executed in Product Explorer with APIs available for third-party applications.

Sales Order Advisor - Guided Sale Orders Creation. Point and click. Product recommendations and add-ons. Tiered package pricings. Discounts prorated across all products. Configured in Prime and executed in Product Explorer with APIs available for third-party applications.

Cloud Cart - More than a cart. Has all the functionality needed to support Big Ticket Order Entry:

  • Customer bill-to and ship-to. Both spouse's/partner's names and contact information.
  • Quotes and leads management.
  • Supports all delivery methods including Take With, Shipped, Drop Shipped and In-Home Delivery.
  • In-Home Delivery booking.
  • Support for multiple financing options. Automatic calculation of payments.
  • If configured, automatic pricing adjustment for cash payments on select products.
  • Automatic calculation of Sale Tax. Support for all US States.
  • Special Allowance including Make the Total Equal function.
  • Automatic calculation of minimum deposit based on product type and delivery method.
  • Automatic calculation of customer savings.
When combined with Sales Order Advisor, orders that used to take a half hour to write up, typically take less than three minutes to complete in Cloud Cart. And no errors.
