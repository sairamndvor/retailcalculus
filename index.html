Our Products

Enterprise-Grade Retail Solutions

All solutions designed and developed from scratch as microservices using open-source technologies

🗃 Core Product

Prime

Product information management system that manages, organizes, enhances and orchestrates all product information including pictures and unstructured data.

💰

Vendis

Sales Associate Selling Suite

  • Product Explorer - An in-store selling web site that delivers all PRIME product information without the need for curation
  • Special Order Configuration - Foolproof point and click special order configuration with automated pricing and purchase order ready text
  • Sales Order Advisor - Guided tool for selling everything from sectionals to patio sets to entire rooms with package pricing
  • Document Viewer - Organized cloud storage for documents such as vendor catalogs and customer agreements
  • Cloud Cart - More than a sales cart. Manages workflow from leads, to quotes to sales. Integration with the store, the web site and the customer
🛠

Sarcio

Work order platform with third-party portal integration for seamless operations.

🚚

Partum

In-home delivery scheduling platform for efficient last-mile logistics.

📊

Optimus

Omni-channel inventory availability platform with real-time rules-based reservation.

🔒

Scout

Provisions and protects all Retail Calculus solutions with enterprise security.
